Mission

DuelGood's mission is to reduce inefficiency and social division by pairing donations made to opposite sides of contested public issues and channeling the opposing funds to outstanding charities.

How It Works

Paired amounts go to GiveWell's Top Charities Fund, high-impact and cost-effective programs that save or improve lives the most per dollar. GiveWell is a highly transparent, reputable, and research-backed organization whose selected charities are the gold standard for impact. More about GiveWell.

The remainder after subtracting the paired amount will still go to its original organization.

For example, say Alice is pro-choice and Bob is pro-life. Alice donates ten dollars to Planned Parenthood, while Bob donates ten dollars to Focus on the Family. These organizations then work against each other, creating social division and waste.

If Alice and Bob had given through DuelGood, then the ten dollars to Planned Parenthood and the ten dollars to Focus on the Family would cancel. GiveWell would receive twenty dollars to save lives, and both Alice and Bob would have advanced their goals by eliminating funding to the opposing cause.

Our operations are funded by donors who choose to support us. 100% of all other donations (minus standard bank and credit card fees) go to charity or the party of your choice.
