Mission

DuelGood's mission is to reduce inefficiency and social division by pairing donations made to opposite sides of contested public issues and channelling the opposing funds to outstanding charities.

For example, if Alice donates $10 to the pro-life Cause A and Bob donates $8 to the pro-choice Cause B, then $8 + $8 = $16 goes to lifesaving charity and the remaining $2 goes to Cause A.

Our operations are funded by donors who choose to support us. 100% of all other donations (minus standard bank and credit card fees) go to charity or the party of your choice.

How It Works

Matched amounts go directly to GiveWell's Top Charities Fund, high-impact and cost-effective programs that save or improve lives the most per dollar. GiveWell is a highly transparent, reputable, and research-backed organization whose selected charities are the gold standard for impact. More about GiveWell.

The remainder will go to whichever cause received more donations of its pair.

Methodology

DuelGood identifies three issue domains characterized by high charitable spending with mutually incompatible objectives.
Issue
Abortion Legal access & reproductive autonomy Prohibition & fetal personhood
Guns Restrictions & regulation for safety Expansive Second Amendment rights
Queer rights Legal equality & recognition Traditional definitions & restrictions
