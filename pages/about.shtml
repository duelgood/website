When you donate to a net-zero cause through DuelGood, your donation is matched with an equal opposing donation and both go to life-saving charities.

For example, if Alice donates $10 to the pro-life Cause A and Bob donates $8 to the pro-choice Cause B, then $8 + $8 = $16 goes to charity and the remaining $2 goes to Cause A.

Our operations are funded by donors who choose to support us. 100% of all other donations (minus standard bank and credit card fees) go to charity or the party of your choice.

Matched amounts go directly to GiveWell's Top Charities Fund, high-impact and cost-effective programs that save or improve lives the most per dollar. GiveWell is a highly transparent, reputable, and research-backed organization whose selected charities are the gold standard for impact. More about GiveWell.

The remainder will go to whichever cause received more donations. This ensures that donating through DuelGood has the same political impact as donating directly to a political party, in addition to saving lives.
