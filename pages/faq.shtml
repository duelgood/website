Frequently Asked Questions

Transparency

How can I verify where my money goes?

We publish detailed monthly reports showing total donations received, amounts sent to each political party, and amounts sent to our chosen charities. Receipts from payment processors and confirmations from recipient organizations are available upon request.

How do you decide which charity to use?

We currently send matched funds to GiveWell's Top Charities Fund. This selection is based on independent, peer-reviewed research into cost-effectiveness and transparency. We reassess annually.

Can you prove you aren’t taking a hidden cut?

Yes. Our operational funding comes from separate, voluntary donations. 100% of all other contributions (minus unavoidable bank/credit card fees) go to charity or the designated political party. Our bank statements and payment processor logs are independently audited.

Security

How do you protect my payment information?

We never store card or bank details on our servers. All transactions are processed through certified, PCI-DSS–compliant payment processors using encrypted connections.

What if someone hacks your site?

We maintain strict security measures, including regular penetration testing, encrypted backups, and isolated systems for sensitive data. Even in a worst-case breach, no payment credentials would be exposed.

Political Neutrality

Are you secretly favoring one political party?

No. Our model is designed to cancel out donations to opposing parties equally. Only the net difference between donations is sent to one party, and the rest is directed to charity.

Why not just give directly to the charity instead of involving politics?

If both sides are giving equally, political donations cancel each other out. By processing through DuelGood, you achieve the same political effect as a direct party donation, while also saving lives with the surplus funds.

Is this even legal under campaign finance laws?

Yes. All political contributions are processed in compliance with relevant laws and regulations, including contribution limits, disclosure requirements, and reporting rules. We verify donor eligibility before processing political donations.

Legal & Tax

Are donations tax-deductible?

Only the portion of your donation that goes to charity may be tax-deductible. The political portion is not. We provide year-end statements showing the split, but we recommend consulting a tax professional.

Can I get a refund if I change my mind?

You can cancel or edit your donation until 7:00 PM Eastern Time on the same day it is made. After that, funds are transmitted and cannot be reversed.

Tough Questions & Criticism

What if your chosen charity wastes money or has a scandal?

If our chosen charity fails to meet our standards for transparency, cost-effectiveness, and impact, we will immediately pause donations to them and redirect to another vetted charity. All changes will be announced publicly.

Isn’t this just a gimmick to make people feel good while still fueling politics?

Our mission is to reduce net-zero political spending waste and redirect surplus funds to save lives. While politics remains involved, the model channels otherwise-wasted dollars toward high-impact outcomes.

Could someone game your system to benefit one side more?

We monitor donation patterns for anomalies and can delay or reject suspicious transactions. However, the core model mathematically limits one-sided exploitation unless the other side stops donating entirely.

Miscellaneous

When are donations processed?

Every day at 7:00 PM Eastern Time. Before that deadline, donations can be edited or canceled.

Can I choose a different charity?

Not at this time. Focusing on a single high-impact fund maximizes transparency, efficiency, and leverage when negotiating fees with processors.
