Legal

Are donations tax-deductible?

Yes, donors can deduct contributions made to DuelGood under IRC Section 170. You'll receive a receipt for tax deduction at the email you provide when donating.

Why doesn't DuelGood have the option to donate to political organizations, advocacy groups, or other non-501(c)(3) entities?

For DuelGood to maintain its 501(c)(3) status and allow donors to claim deductions, the funds must be used for charitable purposes as defined by section 501(c)(3). Passing funds through to 501(c)(4)s, political organizations, or other non-charitable entities would violate this requirement.

Process

How does this work behind the scenes?

When you donate to us with credit, debit, or ACH transfer, your donation goes into DuelGood's bank account. We then send a tax receipt for a deduction to your email. Every month we forward the appropriate amount to each charity via the method with the lowest (typically no) fees.

How did DuelGood choose which charities to feature?

DuelGood identifies three issues characterized by high charitable spending with mutually incompatible objectives.

  • Abortion access
  • Gun access
  • Queer rights
For each area, we picked large, well-known charities whose work opposes one another. In the future we may add more cause areas or change charities.

Transparency

How can I verify my donations actually go where you say?

DuelGood will publish receipts received from each charity for the amount donated each month.

Is DuelGood biased?

Hopefully not. DuelGood is designed to be as neutral as possible, and has no control over how funds are distributed beyond selecting charities.

Is my personal information secure?

Extremely. All personal information you provide is stored with Stripe, a trusted payment provider with the highest level of certification in the industry.

Can I get a refund?

DuelGood is currently working to draft a donation refund policy and establish an easier refund process. In the future, you'll likely be able to refund a donation up until the end of the month. For now, please contact us before the end of the month when the donation was initiated and we'll work to issue a refund, provided the total value of the donation was under $600.
