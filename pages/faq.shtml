Legal

Are donations tax-deductible?

Yes. You'll receive a receipt for tax deduction at the email you provide when donating.

Is what DuelGood does legal?

Yes.

There's plenty of diametrically opposed groups that aren't charities. Why doesn't DuelGood have the option to donate to political organizations, advocacy groups, or other non-501(c)(3) entities?

For DuelGood to maintain its 501(c)(3) status and allow donors to claim deductions, the funds must be used for charitable purposes as defined by section 501(c)(3). Passing funds through to 501(c)(4)s, political organizations, or other non-charitable entities would violate this requirement.

Process

How does this work behind the scenes?

When you donate to us with credit, debit, or ACH transfer, your donation goes into DuelGood's bank account. We then send a tax receipt for a deduction to your email. On the first of each month we calculate how much each cause received in the last month and send the appropriate amount to each charity.
