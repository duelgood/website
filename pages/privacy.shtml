Data We Collect

When you make a donation, we collect the following information:
  • Donated amount.
  • Display name.
  • Preference, 'A' or 'B'.
  • Full legal name.
  • Email.
  • Address.
  • State.
  • ZIP.
  • Employer.
  • Occupation.

Why We Collect Data

  • Your preferred display name is used for leaderboard statistics on our website.
  • Your email is needed to deliver receipts for tax deductions.
  • Federal law requires us to use our best efforts to collect and report the name, mailing address, occupation and the name of the employer of individuals whose contributions exceed $200 in a calendar year.
  • We use your state on our homepage, to display what each state has donated.

How We Use Your Data

  • We store all the information listed above in our secure servers.
  • We will use your email to contact you with information regarding your donations, e.g. donation failed to process, notification of a recurring donation, donation cancellations, tax information, etc.
  • We never sell or share your personal information with anyone except the government in event we are audited.
