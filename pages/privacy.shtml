Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 3, 2025

When you donate through DuelGood, you're trusting us with your personal information. We understand this is a big responsibility and work hard to protect your information.

Contents

  1. What Information We Collect
  2. Why We Collect Information
  3. How We Use Your Information

What Information We Collect

When you make a donation, we collect the following:
  • Donation amount.
  • Charity preference.
  • Display name.
  • Email address.
  • Legal name.
  • Street address.
  • State.
  • ZIP.

Why We Collect Information

  • We need your donation amount and charity preference to route your donation correctly.
  • We deliver tax deductions receipts to your email.
  • Federal law requires us to collect the name and address of donors.
    Section 501(c)(3) organizations (including section 4947(a)(1) nonexempt charitable trusts and nonexempt private foundations described in section 6033(d)) and section 527 political organizations must report the names and addresses of their contributors in Part I, column (b), on Schedule B.

How We Use Your Information

  • We store all the information listed above in our secure servers.
  • We will use your email to contact you with information regarding your donations, e.g. donation failed to process, donation cancellations, tax deduction information.
  • We show your display name on our website.
  • We use your state when displaying the total amount donated by each state.
  • We never sell or share your personal information with anyone except the federal government when required.

DuelGood aims to be as transparent as possible. If you have any inquires regarding your personal information, please visit our contact page and reach out.
