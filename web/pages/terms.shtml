1. Purpose of Service

DuelGood enables users to offset donations to opposing charities and direct the net difference to life-saving causes.

  • Equal amounts to opposing parties are canceled out.
  • The difference is donated to GiveWell's Top Charities Fund (or another high-impact charity designated by DuelGood).
  • The remainder (if any) from each pair is sent to the charity in the pair that received more donations.

Transparency: DuelGood takes no cut. 100% of donations, minus standard bank or card processing fees, are passed through to the designated recipients.
