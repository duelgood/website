Contact

For inquiries, reach out to contact@duelgood.org.

DuelGood does not currently have a bug bounty program. However, if you discover a serious security vulnerability, please report it to contact@duelgood.org and we'd be happy to provide whatever small token of appreciation our budget allows in addition to crediting you on our site.

To suggest improvements unrelated to security, such as translations, accessibility features, or efficiency tweaks, please open an issue on our GitHub.

If you prefer to leave feedback anonymously, please use this form.
