About DuelGood

DuelGood redirects donations to opposing political causes into life-saving charities instead, so donors can neutralize their opponent's funding while supporting high-impact work.

When you donate to a cause through DuelGood, your donation is paired with an equal donation to the opposite side. Those paired amounts are redirected to GiveWell's Top Charities Fund, which supports extremely cost-effective programs that save lives. If one side donates more than the other, any unpaired money still goes to the original intended organization.

For example, imagine Alice donates $100 to Planned Parenthood, while Bob donates $100 to Focus on the Family. Normally, these donations would fund opposing advocacy efforts that largely cancel each other out in the political arena. Through DuelGood, that $200 is redirected to GiveWell's Top Charities instead and will provide malaria prevention for approximately 80 children, or vitamin A supplementation that could save 2-3 children's lives. Both Alice and Bob get what they actually want most: stopping $100 from going to a cause they oppose. And instead of funding a political stalemate, their donations save lives.

Our operations are funded by donors who choose to support us. 100% of all other donations (minus standard bank and credit card fees) go to charity or the party of your choice.
